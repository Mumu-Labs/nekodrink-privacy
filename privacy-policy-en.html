🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

NekoDrink (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us" or "this App") highly values your privacy and personal information security. This Privacy Policy aims to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

📋 Information Collection

We are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This App strictly adheres to the following principles:

  • No personal identity information collected: We do not collect personal identity information such as your name, email, phone number, etc.
  • Local storage only: All data is stored only on your device locally and will not be uploaded to any server
  • Data usage transparency: We clearly state what data we collect and how we use it

📱 App Usage Data

To provide services, this App may collect the following usage data:

  • Drinking records: Including drinking time and amount
  • App settings: Including reminder times, target water intake, notification settings, etc.
  • Device information: Including device model, OS version, App version, etc. (only for troubleshooting)

💾 Data Storage

Your data security is our top priority:

  • Data is stored on your local device using secure storage mechanisms
  • We do not upload data to any server or cloud storage
  • Data is used only for App feature-related purposes
  • You can clear App data at any time (by uninstalling the App or clearing App data)

🔔 Permission Usage

This App may require the following permissions, used only for specified features:

  • Notification permission: For sending drinking reminder notifications
  • Vibration permission: For vibration feedback during reminders
  • Alarm permission: For setting precise reminder times

🚫 Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising services
  • No social media integration
  • No cloud storage services

👶 Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy very seriously:

  • This App is not intended for or directed to children under 13 years of age
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will delete it immediately
  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child is using this App, please contact us to delete any related data

👤 Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Modify App settings
  • Clear all App data
